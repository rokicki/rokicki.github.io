Howdy, Neighbors!

I would be delighted to put a Loma Verde page up here, including support for things like chat boards, mailing lists, pictures, and the like. (And improve the domain name!)

If you think this is a good idea or have suggestions, send me email at rokicki@cs.stanford.edu.

Possible sections include:

  • Meet your neighbors! A collection of photos of people who live and play here.
  • Helpful hints. Things you've found to work you'd like to share. Fans, awnings, storage, good contractors, etc.
  • Discussion board. People can start topics of conversation, post messages, etc.
  • A mailing list. For those missives that need to get immediate attention, or maybe just pointers to the discussion board.
  • Classifieds. Got a teen who can babysit? Need someone to water your plants for a week?
  • Lots more. The web is a blast!
